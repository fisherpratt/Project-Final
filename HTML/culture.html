Culture

What makes Sierra Leone Unique?

Sierra Leone has a goring population of 8.421 million as of 2021 distributed across 10 major cities, Freetown being the largest, and several smaller ones. With it's population comes many different approaches to lifestyles, and traditions.

Religion

    Islam
    • 78.5%
    Christianity
    • 20.4%
    None
    • 1.1%

Language

    English
    • Although many languages exist in Sierra Leone, English is the national language and is very commonly known.
    Krio
    • Roughly 97% of the population speaks this language. This language comes from the descendents of slaves from the west indies who mostly live in Freetown.
    Mende
    • About 31% of the population speak this dialect
    Temme
    • About 37% of the population speak this dialect
    Others
    • Kono, Kissi, Kuranko, Limba, Fula, Mandingo, Susu
