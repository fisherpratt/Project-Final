Geography

Where is Sierra Leone and what is is like?
    Sierra Leone can be found on the west cost of Africa in between Liberia and Guinea. Becasue of this, it's capital, Freetown, is a port city right on the coast.


    Climate

    Sierra Leone's temperature can be considered tropical, with the coastal areas having hotter more humid tempertaures than the inland areas. The average temperature is about 80 degrees farhenheit, and the average rainfall is about 108 inches per year.


    Wildlife

    Sierra Leone is home to a variety of different plant, and animal species. IT is estimated to hold roughly 2100 plant species, 147 species of mammals, 170 species of birds, 70 species of reptiles, 35 amphibian species, and 100 species of fish.
