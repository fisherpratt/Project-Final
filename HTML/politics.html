Where does Sierra Leone stand politically?

As of March 31 2018, Julius Maada Bio has been the president of Sieera Leone.

Sierra Leone's government is established by it's constituation and is located centrally in the city of frettown, the nation's captial.

As written in it's consitution, the government is divided into three seperate branches much like the United States. The branches include Executive, Judicial, and Legislative.

Sierra Leone has 3 main rual provinces, and uses it's capital as an administrative province. After this jursidiction comes 149 chiefdoms.
